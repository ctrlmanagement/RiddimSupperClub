The Patio

Atlanta's most coveted outdoor lounge experience. Covered, ventilated, and charged with the energy of the night — the Patio is where the evening opens up. Hookah coils, cocktails arrive, conversations deepen. The city hums around you but you're somewhere apart.

The Dining Room

An intimate supper club in the truest sense. Low lighting, curated playlists, white tablecloth service — dinner at RIDDIM is an event in itself. Our executive chef builds a menu around seasonal ingredients and the culture of the room. Arrive hungry. Leave satisfied in every sense.

🍽️
Dinner Service

Wine & Cocktails

Live Programming
Jazz and R&B sets during dinner. Transitions to the floor later in the evening.

The Floor

When the lights drop and the bassline takes over, the Floor is where Atlanta's night life becomes art. Resident DJs and guest selectors curate hip-hop and Afrobeats sets that command the room. VIP bottle service, reserved tables, and a sound system built for it.

Resident DJs
